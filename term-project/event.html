Our Annual Event is Here!!!

Cycle the Continental Divide with us...

Our annual event is a 3 Day course that lets you experience the entirety of the Bozeman, Montana Mountains. You'll be able to to experience the Mystic Lake, go around the Grassy Mountain Loop and finish off the 3rd day with Chestnut Mountain.

  • Dates: 4/20-4/22 ALL Day
  • Route:
    • Day 1: Mystic Lake - 18 miles
    • Day 2: Grassy Mountain Loop - 17 miles
    • Day 3: Chestnut Mountain - 15 miles
  • Costs: $300
  • Requirements: Must be an Intermediate Bicyclist. Must have your own bike and equipment. All bicyclists are REQUIRED to carry a First-Aid pack during the entirety of the trip. Lodging will be available all nights, but booking must be made ahead. NO Walk-Ins. Breakfast is FREE in the mornings. There is rest stops with food available for sale along the way as well. It is recommended you bring any extra food you think you might need.
