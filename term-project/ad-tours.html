Daily Adventures

Early Birds get the Worm:

This is a guided tour with our very own Justin Wilcox who has climbed this mountain since his childhood. He will guide you to his personal favorite spot to watch that beautiful Bozeman, Montana sunrise.

  • Length:: 6 miles
  • Skill: Intermediate
  • Cost: $45/person flat
  • Location: Garnett Mountain

It's Leg Day!

This is a guided tour with an experienced bicyclist and native, Kellie June. She will give you an experience of a life time. Be sure to keep up! She tells stories at rest points and they are something not to miss!

  • Length: 9 miles
  • Skill: Beginners/Intermediate
  • Cost: $30/person flat
  • Location: Lick Creek

Summer Adventures

The End of the Golden Road:

Some consider this the best ride in Montana. There is a stiff, 2k ft climb to a gorgeous high alpine lake. Ofcourse, this makes for a killer downhill on the return. It's the most popular tour we have during our summer months. It is a single track and goes up to 7,406 ft.

  • Length: 10 miles
  • Skill: Intermediate
  • Cost: $50/person flat
  • Location: Emerald Lake

Winter Adventures

Badda Bing Badda Boom!:

This trail is best from South to North. It starts out with plenty of switchbacks that will get your lungs and legs warmed up! From there, it levels out with beautiful views and you will continue with classic XC riding wtih ups and downs. There is some more climbing towards the end, but then you have several miles of down, so enjoy!

  • Length: 24 miles
  • Skill: Intermediate
  • Cost: $50/person flat
  • Location: Bangtail Divide
