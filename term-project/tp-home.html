About Us

We love the long and winding trail as much as you do. We cater to your every need as you get ready to embark on your adventure. We have tours that will give you the best views and trails Bozeman, Montana has to offer. Go take a look at our events that happen monthly, anually and just because we love a good time! We constantly have sales on the top-brand items to make your adventure as smooth as possible. We sell parts, equipment and attire. We provide services for your ride, because we know it's "More than just a Bicycle" and we'd love to help take care of it with you. You can contact us 24/7 by email and we're available by telephone every Monday-Saturday 6am-9pm.
